## ğŸ® Gamification Framework

### 1. **Progression System**

#### Experience Points (XP)

- **Knowledge Consumption**: Reading articles (5-50 XP based on difficulty)
- **Skill Validation**: Completing quizzes (25-100 XP)
- **Content Creation**: Submitting protocols (100-500 XP)
- **Peer Review**: Validating submissions (50-200 XP)
- **Deployment**: Field implementation reports (500-2000 XP)
- **Collaboration**: Co-authoring content (bonus multiplier)

#### Level System (1-100)

- **Novice** (1-10): Learning fundamentals
- **Practitioner** (11-25): Applying knowledge
- **Expert** (26-50): Leading projects
- **Master** (51-75): Innovating solutions
- **Guardian** (76-100): Mentoring & governance

#### Skill Trees (per Domain)

- Atmospheric Restoration (5 branches, 25 skills each)
- Biological Systems (5 branches, 25 skills each)
- AI Orchestration (4 branches, 20 skills each)
- Energy Systems (4 branches, 20 skills each)
- Governance (3 branches, 15 skills each)
